Welcome to Middleton HOSA!

HOSA - Future Healthcare Professionals is an international student organization recognized by the U.S. Department of Education, with a mission to promote career opportunities in the health care industry and to enhance the delivery of quality health care to all people. Middleton HOSA is one of the largest HOSA chapters in the entire State of Florida, and its members consistently wins numerous awards at regional, state, and international conferences. HOSA provides a unique program of leadership development, motivation, and recognition for students who have interests in pursuing careers in health professions.
